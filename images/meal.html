Green Bean and Plum Salad

Calories: 2500

Protein: 100g | Carbs: 200g | Fat: 105g

Ingredients:

Green Beans, Plums, Olive Oil, Garlic, Salt, Pepper

Instructions:

1. Blanch green beans. 2. Slice plums. 3. Toss with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper. 4. Serve chilled.

Diet:

Vegan, Gluten-Free
